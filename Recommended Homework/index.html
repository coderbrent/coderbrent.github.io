About Me


Background

I was born and raised in a rather lousy part of Staten Island, New York. While my family never had the means to provide me with Air Max 95's or Nautica jeans growing up, I believe my humble background shaped many of the qualities I am proudest of as the person I am today.

In my late teens/early twenties, I bounced around in the retail circuit for a few years, but personal computing was always my full time hobby. Whether I was tinkering with eggdrop bots on IRC, trying to get +v in #Razor1911 or learning network security as a script kiddy and defacing the Chinese US Embassy website, I've always had a passion for computers.

I also own and operate an award winning limousine service in Red Bank, NJ called Red Bank Limo that generates $1m/year in annual revenue.
