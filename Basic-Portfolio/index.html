About Me

Background

Born and raised on the North Shore of Staten Island, NY, while my family never had the means to provide me with Air Max 95's or Nautica jeans growing up, I believe my humble background shaped many of the qualities I am proudest of as the person I am today.

Professional

As I bounced around retail jobs in my late teens/early twenties, personal computing was always my full time hobby. Whether I was tinkering with an eggdrop bot on IRC, kissing up to the guys in Razer1911 or rooting and defacing the Chinese US Embassy website (seriously, I think attrition.org still mirrors an archive of our groups hacks) I couldn't get away from my passion for programming.

I also started, operated an sold an award winning limousine service in 2012 that was operating at $1m/yearly revenue.
